The content here is not obsured by the x-cabnet element due to the ghost attribute.







These two cabinets are blocking and preserve thier layout open or closed, as they are not ghost clicking anywhere in the potential space will trigger the open event

right opening cabinet left opening cabinet

You may or may not have noticed that the left cabinet only responds to dragging the handle, and the right only responds to clicking / tapping. be default all cabinets are clickable. draggable cabinets must not be inline and have `draggable="true"`. you can turn these default interaction off using the attributes `clickable` and `draggable`

This cabinet is inline and ghost and will concede its shape when closed allowing other elements to flow around it like a float
